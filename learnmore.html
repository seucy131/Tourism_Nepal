INTRODUCTION

Tourism is a social,cultural and economic phenomenon which entails the movement of people to countries or places outside their usual environment for person or business/professional purposes. These people are called visitors(which may be either tourists or excursionists ; residents or non-residents ). Tourism is the activities of people travelling to staying in places outside their usual environment for leisure, business or other purpose for not more than one consecutive year.

Nepal is famous all over the world for it's unique and artistic historical and religious sites. Nepal is a multi-religion in society.The major religion in Nepal is Hinduism,and the pashupatinath temple, which is the world's one of the main Hindu religious sites is located in Kathmandu, attracts many pilgrims and tourism. Kathmandu the capital city of Nepal is the city of temple and very rich in cultural heritage. Within 7 Km radious there are 7 world heritages sites which cannot be found anywhere.
Nepal with rich ancient cultures set against the most dramatic scenery in the world is a land of discovery and unique experience full of art, architecture and culture ,the Kathmandu valley is the prime destination for the tourist.As Nepal is a land of festivities and celebrations, visitors can experience how a culturally diverse and rich society of Nepal grooms in harmony.

Buddhism is another main religion. The world heritage sites Lumbini ,which is the birthplace of Gautam Buddha ,is an important pilgrimage Buddhist site is swayambhunath , the monkey temple in Kathmandu. Tourism in Nepal is also boosted with the adventure activities like; ice-climbing , trekking, hiking,rock climbing, paragliding,jeep flyer, bungee jumping, jungle safari, and more.For those who love adventure travel,Nepal offers endless options along with the beautiful views of the landscapes.Nepal tourism is also highly popular due to the highest mountain peak of world the Mount Everest.And there are many other activities which attracts tourists in Nepal .

Importance of tourism

The important of tourism comes to light since it brings in lot of advantage with it to any host county.It promotes the country name around the globe. It can be great sources of income for a country. Switzerland, France,Italy and Spain and many other countries today are counted as flourished nations and the main reasons behind this is the development of tourism industry. Nepal each year gets a lot of visits and is in the path of tourism development by pre-planning events and activities to allure tourists. Below are the few points for the important of tourism in Nepal:

Economic growth:


For the developing countries like Nepal, the tourism industry can be regarded as bliss as it is a source of foreign exchange and royalty earnings. Tourism succors others industries like hotels , restaurant, and many more to run in a effective way.

Employment opportunities:


Tourism and hospitality also support indirect employment in activities like restaurant supplies, construction company that build and maintain tourists facility, marketing agencies,etc.

Cultural exchange:


Tourists from various countries with different backgrounds visit Nepal. In addition to that, Nepal is a home of people with unlike cultural frameworks from being multilingual to multiregional. Hence people can know each other more and learn about their language,art,skill,food,etc.

Publicity of nation:


Nepal is a small county and has not remarkable name in the world scenario. Tourism helps to publicize the country around the world so that people can know Nepal and it's authentic beauty. It helps to publicize Nepalese art, skills, traditional, culture and hospitality around the world.

Problems of Tourism

Though tourism industry has a very high potential. However, Nepal is not able to sufficiently develop this sector. And the problem/ changelles of tourism are:

  • Poor transportation and communication facilities:


    Transportation and communication are the two main pillars of a modern era. These facilities are required for the development of the tourism sector. But there is lack of modern transportation and communication facility in Nepal. Due to it tourism sector is not well developed in Nepal .

  • Lack of recreation facilities:


    Recreation is an important part of human life. In Nepal; means of recreation like clubs,park, rafting, boating,etc are very limited. Therefore, the average length of stay at present for a tourists in Nepal is only about 13 days.

  • Lack of development of tourists sites:


    Really speaking Nepal is full and preasent places. Rara, Dhorpatan, hile, Shreenagar, etc are the examples. But this places are not yet develop as tourists sites. Thus, tourist visiting Nepal return back to their country by staying only in Pokhara, Kathmandu, chitwan.

  • Lack of tourists center and security:


    Tourism centres have been established in Nepal only in limited places. These centers provide different tourism services like information about pleasant places, tourism maps, exchanging foreign currency etc for tourists. Due to limited tourists centers, there is possiblities For tourists to be cheated. In some places tourists have been robbed. Thus lack of tourism centers and proper security arrangements are a great deterrent to the development of tourism in Nepal .

  • Lack of quality of hotels:


    Good qualyof hotels and lodges are the choice of tourists. But, such good quality hotels and lodges are limited numbers in Nepal. Most of these are concentrated only in Kathmandu valley which limits/stops foreign tourists only in Kathmandu valley.
